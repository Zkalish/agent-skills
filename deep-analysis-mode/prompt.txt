# Bloomberg-Style Deep Analysis Prompt

## Input Format
```
<company_or_asset>ISGSY</company_or_asset>
<market>Public</market>
<time_horizon>Medium</time_horizon>
<investor_type>Growth</investor_type>
<region_optional>Turkey</region_optional>
```

## Operating Rules
1. No generic advice
2. No hype language
3. Flag uncertainty explicitly
4. Separate facts from assumptions
5. Write like capital is at risk

## Output Sections
1. Executive Snapshot (1 paragraph)
2. Business & Market Model
3. Financial Model (Base/Bull/Bear)
4. Narrative Map
5. Risk Register (Severity/Likelihood/Detectability)
6. Competitive Matrix
7. Investor Decision Frame
8. Final Verdict (Buy/Watch/Avoid + Confidence)

## Example Output Structure

### SECTION_1: EXECUTIVE SNAPSHOT
[1 paragraph summary]

### SECTION_2: BUSINESS AND MARKET MODEL
- Revenue model
- Key drivers
- Cost structure
- TAM/SAM/SOM

### SECTION_3: FINANCIAL MODEL
| Scenario | Revenue Growth | Margin | Multiple |
|----------|---------------|--------|----------|
| Base | x% | y% | zx |
| Bull | x+x% | y+y% | z+zx |
| Bear | x-x% | y-y% | z-zx |

### SECTION_4: NARRATIVE MAP
| Case | Scenario | Probability |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Bull | [story] | xx% |
| Base | [story] | xx% |
| Bear | [story] | xx% |

### SECTION_5: RISK REGISTER
| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Detectability |
|------|----------|------------|---------------|
| [Risk 1] | H/M/L | H/M/L | H/M/L |
| [Risk 2] | H/M/L | H/M/L | H/M/L |

### SECTION_6: COMPETITIVE MATRIX
| Factor | [Stock] | [Competitor 1] | [Competitor 2] |
|--------|---------|----------------|----------------|
| Valuation | x | y | z |
| Growth | x% | y% | z% |
| ROE | x% | y% | z% |

### SECTION_7: INVESTOR DECISION FRAME
- **Investable if:** [conditions]
- **Trap indicators:** [red flags]
- **Decision change signal:** [what would flip the view]
- **Key metric:** [the number that matters most]

### SECTION_8: FINAL VERDICT
| Stance | Watch |
| Confidence | Medium |
| Stronger if: | [catalysts needed] |
